easts membership

All of our players and people participating in coaching are required to be members of both easts volleyball and Volleyball Queensland. The membership with Volleyball Queensland is one of the requirements of our affiliation and ensures that our members are covered by insurance when participating in any easts or VQ events.

To become a member of easts volleyball, follow this process:

  1. Check the status of your registration with Volleyball Queensland by contacting VQ
  2. If you aren't currently registered or need to renew, you can do through this link. Please make sure that you select the right type of membership for the competition/event that you are participating in. If you're unsure please contact us via facebook.
  3. Complete the membership application form to the right
  4. We will contact you to confirm your membership within 72 hours

transferring to another club

Here's the process that you need to follow to transfer to another club.

  1. Contact us to check whether you have any fees outstanding as we can't approve a transfer until all outstanding fees are paid
  2. Download and complete the VQ Club Release / Transfer Form (link is available to the right) - please make sure that it is completed fully before sending it through to us
  3. We will do a final check to make sure there are no fees outstanding - if there are we will contact you
  4. If all fees are fully paid, we will sign the form and send it through to both you and the member database manager email address at VQ

VQ Club Release / Transfer form

We will process your transfer forms within 72 hours. Please make sure that you check any VQ imposed deadlines on transfers as missing these deadlines may mean that you cannot participate in a competition or competition round.
