easts membership

All of our players and people participating in coaching are required to be members of both easts volleyball and Volleyball Queensland. The membership with Volleyball Queensland is one of the requirements of our affiliation and ensures that our members are covered by insurance when participating in any easts or VQ events.

To become a member of easts volleyball, follow this process:

Check the status of your registration with Volleyball Queensland by contacting VQ email db@vq.org.au
If you aren't currently registered or need to renew, you can do so online - use this link. Please make sure that you select the right type of membership for the competition/event that you are participating in. If you're unsure please contact us via facebook. VQ Registration Form
Complete the easts membership application form to the right Easts membership application
We will contact you to confirm your membership within 72 hours

transferring to easts

If you are currently registered with VQ as being a member of another club (even if it was many years ago since you have last played) you will need to be transferred to easts in VQ's records. The main concern is that all of your fees with that club have been paid. This process is provided to assist you.

  1. If you are not sure if you have been registered to another club, please contact VQ and check their records. They will be able to confirm if you need a transfer.
  2. Download and complete the VQ Club Release / Transfer Form (link is available to the right) - please make sure all sections are completed.
  3. Email the form to the club requesting that they complete your transfer.
  4. When the form has been signed by the other club, they should email it to VQ. If you receive an email from that club, check to see if they have sent it to VQ. If not, send it to db@vq.org.au

If you have any problems with this process, please contact us and we will help you out.

transferring to another club

Transfers can only be approved if there are no outstanding fees payable by the player to the club. The process of approving a transfer can take up to 72 hours. Please make sure that you check the day and time that your transfer must be received by VQ as failing to comply with these time limits can mean that you will not be eligible to play in a competition or a competition round. If your request is urgent, please contact us using facebook messenger and we will do our best to process your request as quickly as possible.

Here's the process that you need to follow to transfer to another club.

Contact us to check whether you have any fees outstanding as we can't approve a transfer until all outstanding fees are paid. email db@vq.org.au
Download and complete the VQ Club Release / Transfer Form (link is available to the right) - please make sure that it is completed fully before sending it through to us. VQ Club Release / Transfer form
We will do a final check to make sure there are no fees outstanding - if there are we will contact you.
If all fees are fully paid, we will sign the form and send it through to both you and the member database manager email address at VQ
