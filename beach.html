Beach Volleyball

We love beach volleyball!!

We think learning both beach and indoor skills are great for all volleyballers. They are completely different games, but each supports the other in different ways. There's a bit of transition when moving from one to the other. However, we believe the benefits are worth the effort of adjusting when moving from one season to the other.

Focus of our beach squad

  • Developing sand "legs".
  • Increase fitness, strength, agility and endurance.
  • Develop excellent ball control skills.
  • Develop game strategy and awareness.

Tournaments and Competitions

There are some opportunities for the players to fill in the competition at Sandstorm on the night. There is a league held by Sandstorm on Monday (pairs), Tuesday (pairs and fours) and Wednesday (Fours).

QLD Beach Volleyball Tour: this tour is the highest level of beach volleyball in the state, providing competition for the elite and developing players. It is hosted at a range of venues from the Gold Coast to North Queensland. The tour runs from October 2018 - march 2019.

There are various divisions from Premier - Challenger - Junior divisions.

The Junior teams will also have various qualifying tournaments for the Queensland Junior Beach Volleyball teams that compete in the Australian Junior Beach Volleyball Championships (held at Bunbury, Western Australia from 13 - 18 April 2019). Trials for these teams are held later in 2018 - the date to be confirmed.
