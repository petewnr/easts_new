Senior Premier Volleyball Training Squads

Easts have been involved in PVL since 2004 when we started with just one team.

We are continuing to strive for good representation across the PVL competition divisions. This year, we are aiming for a Grand Prix team in addition to PVL. So this may see some changes to the teams that we enter into PVL.

Key Dates

Volleyball Queensland will finalise the dates shortly and they will be advertised here. The season generally runs for 16 rounds starting in early April and concluding in late August.

Easts trials for PVL are held mid February and further information of costs and details of training sessions will be added as soon as they become available.

Open Social Training Squad

Easts run open social training sessions to allow the beginner adult players to improve their skill level. These players often may not be able to commit to a full season of competitive volleyball and wish to have the flexibility to shorter seasons or a lower level of competition. Players can attend training on a weekly basis but the schedule is flexible to fit into the persons schedules, hence there is no fixed commitment to attend every week.
