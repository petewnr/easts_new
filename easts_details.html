about easts

easts volleyball is an amateur volleyball club based in the east region of Brisbane, Australia. As a club we participate in competitions across a range of age groups and levels, in both indoor and beach volleyball. We provide training and coaching for our teams and players that occur at times aligned to the different competition seasons within our region. Details of the specific competitions and training sessions are available in this website, organised by discipline and then age group.

Our coaches are skilled and experienced, each with many years working with a variety of different levels of players - from people first starting out with volleyball to those aiming to compete in high level competitions.

We are affiliated with Volleyball Queensland and, therefore, all who participate in our coaching or competition teams must also be members of Volleyball Queensland.
